For "Acc" where you find "|":
the first part is used for "me" and singular "you", and the second part is used for the rest
For "(...)", they are prepositions or particles that are used with the verb.
With 'Intr', sometimes Tamazight uses prepostions different than English, so we defined them here as possible.
Example:
I am thinking about = ⴰⵔ ⵙⵡⵉⵏⴳⵉⵎⵖ ⴳ. (we used 'ⴳ' with 'ⵙⵡⵉⵏⴳⵎ')
